THIS IS NOT A TOY

YEAR

2015

FOR

Undergrad Thesis

TAGS

editorial, print, justice, political, protest

A series of two handmade books documenting an eight month long thesis project on issues of discrimination against race, gender and class differences. The books explore personal encounters with these issues, critiques of the media, an analysis of historical protests and finally design's role throughout these moments of social controversy. Compositions are created by juxtaposing the essence of non-human vs human elements. They are honest, exciting and debatable critiques of modern society that beg to ask, what divides us and unites us?
