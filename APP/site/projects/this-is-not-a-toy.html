UNDERGRAD THESIS

2015

A series of two handmade books documenting an eight month long project on the discrimination against racial, gender and class differences. The books explore personal encounters with these issues, critiques of the retinaia, an analysis of historical protests and design's role throughout these moments of social controversy. Compositions are created by juxtaposing the essence of non-human vs human elements. They are honest, exciting and debatable critiques of modern society that beg to ask, what divides us and unites us?
